Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What services does this maternal health app provide?

This app offers registration, personal health tracking, reminders for antenatal care visits, and information about government schemes.

How do I register on the app?

You can register by entering your mobile number and setting a password on the registration page.

When will I start receiving reminders?

Reminders start after you complete your registration and fill out your personal health details.

Can I update my personal information later?

Yes, you can update personal details anytime via the profile page.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, all data is stored securely and used only to provide health services and reminders.

What if I have technical issues or questions?

Contact support via the help section in the app or reach out to your local health worker.
